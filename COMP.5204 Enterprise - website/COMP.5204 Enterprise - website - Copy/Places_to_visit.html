Places that I would like to visit overseas

Bohol

Bohol is one of the provinces of the Phillippines and is well known for the unusual geological formations and coral reefs.
There are tours available in Bohol which takes you to various places such as the Chocolate Hills and the
sancturary of the Tarsier, the smallest monkey in the world.

Maui Ocean Center

The Maui Ocean Center is an aquarium located in Hawaii. It is the largest tropical reef aquarium in the western hemisphere
and holds over thousands of fish and 40 different pacific coral species. They also offer a shark dive activity where
you get to swim up close with sharks.

Akame 48 Waterfalls

The Akame 48 Waterfalls is located in Habari, a city of Japan.
It offers a beautiful trail to hike which leads to the waterfalls.
There is a ninja training school where you can take a 90 minute ninja training course that includes
ninja star throwing, wall climbing and rope traversing.

The Great Barrier Reef

The great barrier reef is located off the coast of Queensland in Australia.
It is the worlds largest coral reef system and is made up of over 2900 reefs and 900 islands.
There are glass bottom boats available hop aboard to view the marine life
as well as scuba diving activities also available.
